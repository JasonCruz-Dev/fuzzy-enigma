Whole Life Insurance

Is a Permanent Life Lnsurance, which means the insured person is covered for the duration of their life as long as premiums are paid on time. Premium amount is locked in once approved. This policy also builds cash value as it accumulates over time. Permanent Life Insurance is different than Term Insurance which covers the insured person for a set amount of time (usually between 10 and 30 years).

Term Insurance

With Term Insurance you can get high coverage for a very low cost. Term coverage options are 10, 15, 20, 25, or 30 years. Price never increases or decreases once the client is approved. Terms can also include living benfits at no additional cost. Which means if you were ever diagnosed with: chronic, critical, or terminal illness, you can accelerate a portion of your death benefit while you are ALIVE!

Final Expense Insurance

For this type of Whole Life Insurance no medical exam is required. As long as you fall between the age limits of 50-90 years old, and you can often get approved for a policy within minutes.
